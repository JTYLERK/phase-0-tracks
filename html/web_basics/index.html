Ode to Cats

Or, man's second best friend


Cats are wonderful pets. Here are the top reasons why:

  1. Cats like to play fetch. Oh wait, no they don't. Scratch that. (pun intended ;D )
  2. Cats love to walk with you in the park. Errr, not really, nevermind this point.
  3. Cats give you unconditional love at all times. Actually, that's not quite true either, sorry about that.
  4. Cats don't tear up your furniture. Well, not all of them. Okay, most of them. Alright, all of them...
  5. Cats don't pee on your new couch to mark it. Damnit, that's wrong too. This totally happened to me three months ago. :S

After taking some time to reflect, here are some more accurate reasons why cats are wonderful pets.

  • Cats have swagger
  • Cats can talk, but choose not to
  • Cats can read your mind, but they don't use that to their advantage...often
  • At one time cats ruled Ancient Egypt, and, by proxy, many fractal galaxies
  • Without cats we wouldn't have catepillars, catalogs, guitars (no catgut) and no hamburgers (no cattle, thus no meat). Surely cats have much to offer us!
