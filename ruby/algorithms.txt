#algorithm for washing hair

wet hair
pour shampoo in hands
lather shampoo in hair
rinse hair with water
	if hair still has shampoo
		- continue to rinse with water
	else 
		- pour conditioner in hand
lather conditioner in hair
rinse hair with water
dry hair
